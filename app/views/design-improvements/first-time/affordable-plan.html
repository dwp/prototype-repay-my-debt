How a more affordable plan works

  • Start your plan by entering an amount you can afford to pay each month

    If your financial situation improves, you should sign in to your account and manage your plan to increase your payments or pay additional amounts.

  • We may review your plan after 12 months

    If you have been unable to increase your payment plan, we will contact you to review your situation and understand how much you can afford.

  • Sign up for notifications

    You can receive reminders that you can manage your plan when your situation changes, take action if there are any problems with payments and be notified of any review dates.
