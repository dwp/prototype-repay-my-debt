Sign in with GOV.UK One Login


You can sign in to your Repay and manage benefit money you owe account using your GOV.UK One Login.

You'll be able to create a GOV.UK One Login if you do not already have one.

GOV.UK One Login allows you to securely access this and other government services that also use GOV.UK One Login.

If you do not already have a GOV.UK One Login

You will need to prove your identity when you create your GOV.UK One Login. This will help keep your information safe.

You will need:

  • a valid photo ID, such as your passport or driving licence
  • your current home address (you will need to provide your previous address if you have moved within the last 3 months)

If you already have a GOV.UK One Login

Sign in using the GOV.UK One Login you previously set up.
