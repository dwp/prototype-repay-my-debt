I need help understanding how much will be taken from my Universal Credit payment

Normally the most that can be taken to repay money you owe is 15% of your Universal Credit Standard Allowance.

You can find out about money taken off your Universal Credit payment (opens in new tab).

Example of standard allowances and monthly repayments

This example does not include:

  • any extra amounts that apply to you
  • any money taken off your payment for Universal Credit advances
How much you'll get Monthly standard allowance Monthly repayment
If you’re single and under 25 £292.11 up to £43.80
If you’re single and 25 or over £368.74 up to £55.30
If you live with your partner and you’re both under 25 £458.51 (for you both) up to £68.80
If you live with your partner and either of you are 25 or over £578.82 (for you both) up to £86.80
