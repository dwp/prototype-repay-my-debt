Help and support

If you're struggling to pay, you can temporarily reduce your payments. Alternatively, you must contact DM Service Centre to discuss the options available to you.

DM Service Centre

  • Telephone: 0800 587 1322
  • Textphone: 0800 587 2986 (for deaf or hard of hearing users and customers with speech difficulties)
  • Monday to Friday, 9am to 4pm (closed on public holidays)
