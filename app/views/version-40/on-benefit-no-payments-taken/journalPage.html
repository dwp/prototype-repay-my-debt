You can use a new service to manage the money you owe to DWP

As you are repaying money to DWP, you can use the new Repay and manage benefit money you owe service.

Go to https://www.gov.uk/repay-manage-benefit-owed for more information.

On the new service you can view a breakdown of what you owe to DWP and manage your repayments.

The money you owe to DWP appears under 'Debts and loans repayments' on your Universal Credit statement where you can also find more information.

If you have any other deductions from your Universal Credit payment, they cannot be managed on the new service. For example, any repayments for an advance taken out during your current claim.

If you have any questions, you should contact DWP Debt Management contact center directly:

Telephone: 0800 916 0647
Textphone: 0800 916 0651
NGT text relay (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 916 0647
