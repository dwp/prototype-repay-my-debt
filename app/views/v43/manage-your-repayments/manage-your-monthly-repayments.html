Manage your monthly repayments

We can help you understand how much you can afford to repay each month. We will ask you some questions to learn more about your financial situation.

What you need to know about using this service

You can:

  1. make changes to your monthly repayments
  2. manage repayments to DWP only - see the ‘Debts and loan repayments’ section of your Universal Credit statement

You cannot:

  1. make additional payments or pay off the amount in full online - you need to call us on the number listed on our help and support page
  2. set up a payment plan - repayments are automatically deducted from your Universal Credit
  3. manage other deductions from your Universal Credit payment such as repayments for advances taken out during your current claim
