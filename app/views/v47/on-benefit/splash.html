What you need to know

This service is for getting help to understand how much you can afford if you are struggling with your repayments.

You can only manage repayments to DWP using this service.

If you have any other deductions from your Universal Credit payment, they cannot be managed on this service. For example, any repayments for an advance taken out during your current claim.
