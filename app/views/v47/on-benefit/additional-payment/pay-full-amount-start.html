If you want to pay off a Universal Credit advance in your current claim, contact DWP Debt Management.

You may have recently received a Universal Credit payment or have one due in the next 7 days. If so, wait for the deduction to appear in your payment history before paying off what you owe in full. This helps you avoid overpaying.
