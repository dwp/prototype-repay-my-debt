You can pay off what you owe in full or make a smaller one-off payment.

If you do this you will clear the total amount you owe.

If you have a Universal Credit payment due within the next 5 working days, wait until you have received it before making your payment. This is to stop you overpaying.

If you do this:

  1. you will reduce the total amount you owe and pay it off quicker
  2. the amount deducted from your monthly Universal Credit payments will remain the same
