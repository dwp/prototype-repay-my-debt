Universal Credit - Overpayment (joint)

Overpayment period
1 April 2015 to 31 March 2016
Original amount owed
£1,500.00
Current amount owed
£1,300.00
Overpayment reason

You were overpaid because you or your partner earned too much from employment, either full time or part time.

You were sent a letter about your overpayment on 5 January 2024.

Money you owe with your partner
This amount is shared with your partner so you're both responsible for the repayments. The total amount will show on your account and also your partner's.

Help and support

You can check your Universal Credit journal if you have access to it for more details, or contact Universal Credit:

  • Telephone: 0800 012 1331
  • Textphone: 0800 012 1441 (for deaf or hard of hearing users and customers with speech difficulties)
  • Monday to Friday, 10am to 4pm (closed on bank holidays)
