Help and support

If you're struggling to pay, you can temporarily reduce your payments. Alternatively, you must contact DWP Debt Management to discuss the options available to you.

DWP Debt Management

  • Telephone: 0800 158 5519
  • Textphone: 18001 then 0800 158 5519 when using Relay UK (opens in new tab)
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 7:30pm (closed on public holidays)

Find out about call charges (opens in a new tab)

Further advice

If you would like independent advice, you can contact:

  • MoneyHelper (opens in new tab)
  • Citizens Advice (opens in new tab)
  • Civil Legal Advice (opens in new tab)
  • StepChange Debt Charity (opens in new tab)
