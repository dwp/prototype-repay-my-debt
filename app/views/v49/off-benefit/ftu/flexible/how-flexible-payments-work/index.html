How flexible payments work

Flexible payments work in the following way:

  • Make a card payment to set up your flexible plan
  • Your monthly target is £150.00 but you can pay more if you want to
  • You can make multiple payments throughout the months long as you pay at least £150.00 every month
  • You can contact us for help if you're struggling to pay

You must pay at least £150.00 every calender month, including for March 2026
