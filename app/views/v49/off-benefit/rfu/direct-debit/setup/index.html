Direct Debit payments

If you want to set up a monthly Direct Debit online:

  • you must be an account holder and be able to authorise Direct Debits with your bank or building society
  • you will need your bank or building society account details
  • you will also need your sort code and account number if you have a building society account

Once your plan is set up, you can sign in to manage your plan, change payments, pay additional amounts or get help and support.

For joint account holders:

If you have a joint account and you and your partner both need to authorise Direct Debits, you can set up a Direct Debit by contacting Debt Management:

DWP Debt Management

  • Telephone: 0800 158 5519
  • Textphone: 18001 then 0800 158 5519 when using Relay UK (opens in new tab)
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 7:30pm (closed on public holidays)
