You cannot set up a Direct Debit online


You must be an account holder and be able to authorise Direct Debits with your bank or building society.

If you have a joint account and you and your partner both need to authorise Direct Debits, you can set up a Direct Debit by contacting DWP Debt Management.

Alternatively, you must contact DWP Debt Management to discuss the options available to you.

DWP Debt Management

  • Telephone: 0800 158 5519
  • Textphone: 18001 then 0800 158 5519 when using Relay UK (opens in new tab)
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 7:30pm (closed on public holidays)
